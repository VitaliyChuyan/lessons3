In 2022, the Frankivsk Half Marathon team expresses its sincere thanks to the Armed Forces of Ukraine, the Territorial Defense of the Armed Forces of Ukraine and the National Guard of Ukraine, to all defenders who defend and liberate our lands.

We are announcing a fundraiser to support the ASKOLD Special Training Center for our military personnel. Today, the center continues to build up the material and technical base, to master new areas of training, so that our boys and girls are always ready!

  2. 10km

At the Frankivsk Half Marathon'22, each participant will receive a starting number, a chip for the electronic system of recording results and a finisher's medal, as well as gifts from our partners companies Molokiya, TM Rybak, Newtons, Esculab medical laboratory and others.

Route map FHM'22

We run along a beautiful route that covers a picturesque lake and historic streets of charming Ivano-Frankivsk. The harmonious alternation of nature and the stone jungle of our route will contribute to a very close acquaintance with the city. The pleasant freshness of the lake and the coziness of the center will leave only positive feedback in your soul. A simple route of two laps will allow you to show yourself in all your running glory and improve your time, and possibly set a personal best.
